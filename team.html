Meet Our Engineers

Robert Marcus Jr. Born on November 16, 1967, in the serene rural town of Östersund, Sweden, Robert Marcus is a seasoned civil and structural engineer with over three decades of global experience in the design and construction of complex infrastructure, particularly in the energy sector. As the Chief Executive Officer of Crestline Constructions, Robert brings a unique blend of technical mastery, visionary leadership, and a deeply rooted commitment to sustainable and high-impact development.

Raised in a humble household by Swedish parents who instilled in him the values of integrity, perseverance, and community service, Robert spent his formative years immersed in the natural beauty and resourcefulness of rural Sweden. His fascination with how things were built, From bridges to barns, Grew into a passionate pursuit of engineering excellence.

At college age, Robert relocated to the United Kingdom to broaden his academic and cultural horizons. He earned his Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from the University of Liverpool, where he distinguished himself both academically and through hands-on project involvement. During his time in England, he also met his late wife, with whom he shares a strong partnership rooted in mutual support and shared values.

Keen to deepen his expertise, Robert moved to the United States, completing a Master’s degree in Structural Engineering at a leading institution Nova Southeastern University in Florida. His graduate work focused on large-scale industrial structures, with a growing interest in the challenges of designing safe, efficient, and environmentally conscious gas processing plants, A specialization that would become central to his career.

Over the years, Robert has overseen the engineering and construction of major gas facilities across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and parts of Asia, often working in challenging environments requiring innovation, resilience, and cross-cultural collaboration. His work has contributed to energy infrastructure that supports millions of people, balancing technical excellence with a deep understanding of regional needs.

As CEO of Crestline Constructions, Robert leads with the same values he grew up with, purpose, precision, and people first thinking. Under his leadership, Crestline has grown into a respected international firm known for designing and building critical infrastructure with a commitment to safety, sustainability, and long-term value.

His personal journey from the forests of Östersund to the boardrooms of international construction, shapes his vision for Crestline: to engineer infrastructure that empowers communities, respects the environment, and sets new standards for excellence in civil and structural engineering.

Robert remains deeply grounded despite his global achievements. A devoted husband and father of one, he prioritizes family and believes that the strength of a community begins at home. He is also an advocate for young engineers, mentoring future professionals and championing education in STEM fields, particularly in underserved areas.

A passionate traveller, Robert has visited over 20 countries, often combining his professional projects with cultural exploration. He credits his international experiences with giving him a broader perspective on human needs and the transformative power of well designed infrastructure.
